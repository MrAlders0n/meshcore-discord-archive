I haven't implemented phone calls just yet, so just SMS for now. Phone calls is something I'm still looking into but definitely hope to try and implement in future. If you hold down the user button (top one on the side) and then press the reset button briefly that should put it in DFU mode for flashing. Don't forget to format your SD card to FAT32. You can either use the Meshcore web flasher and choose the Custom Firmware option to flash the bin file- or if you wanted to have it alongside ripple on the Launcher, just copy the bin file to your SD card on your computer, then put the SD card back into your TD Pro. Then in Launcher mode on the Pro tap on the SD menu, tap the name of the firmware you want, and tap Install. If you haven't used Launcher much, fair warning that it'll say Install every time you want to swap between firmwares, regardless of whether you've installed that firmware previously.
