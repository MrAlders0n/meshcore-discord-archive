Mike's Allotment 2026-03-10 7:16 a.m.
The existing MC Heltec V2 firmware works fine - so you shouldn't be needing to change anything. There is a known 'feature' with the sx1276 radio where it suffers from frequency shift/offset depending on which radio params you're using. I've posted many times previously about this - just search for posts by me with Heltec V2. Basically you might just need to configure the frequency slightly higher. Might not be your issue, but worth a try...
