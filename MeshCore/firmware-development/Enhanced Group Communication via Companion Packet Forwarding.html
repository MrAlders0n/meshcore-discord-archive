Proposal: 'Last hop(e)' function to expand local coverage and allow creation of small meshes. For large meshes this will allow to connect devices with shitty antennas to clients that have repeater in range. Lost client will choose one of clients as access point to repeater. All comms will go through this client, to minimize broadcast clog. When device gets in range of repeater, switch to normal Client mode. In this scenario Lost Client should be aware of who have repeater access to reach global network. And Client that used as router might need to be aware of Lost. Lost dont rebroadcast messages, only receive in big network. Small mesh: every client in Lost mode. No repeaters around, and with software constraint on visible node count we can activate broadcast for messages, to allow meshtastic-like operation for group of people hiking or riding on bikes in distant areas, without need to configure anything separately.
