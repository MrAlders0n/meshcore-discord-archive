PiGuy9614 2026-03-31 1:57 p.m.
Max radius is dynamic based on what the antenna height is set to. If the antenna height is 5 meters for example, it won’t realistically/effectively hit pretty much anywhere 11km away as the crow flies. We were able to increase that dynamically. There is a repeater in my area that is 68meters high, so our radius for that maxed out at 34km. Pretty much just depends on what the radio horizon would realistically be. The lead dev implemented that functionality, so I am not as great at explaining it, but that’s the gist
