About Narrow and why.... In a nutshell what this does is reduce our bandwidth to 62.5khz vs the 250khz currently used. This allows us to move off the center frequency off 869.525 mhz in the UK where we know there is a lot of activity from general ISM devices. (Streetlight systems, smart meters etc) which can cause havoc with the mesh and increase the noisefloor to really bad levels. The radio preset is the new EU/UK Narrow preset in the app. (edited)

Main advantages of narrow are:
  • Narrower bandwidth so it can avoid interference.
  • Virtually the same performance as current settings but faster and in some cases more reliable. (weak links often improved) Reduces collisions.
  • Allows for 4 channels in the EU allocation.
  • Reduces perceptive noise floor. This is subjective.
  • This has been tried and tested in difficult areas like USA (die hard seattle mesh) and Bratislava with very bad RF interference on the center frequency of 869.525
