I saw a case here the other day where Andy who has a direct strong SNR signal to me sent a message that arrived via 18 hops. This is rare as it's normally a single hop but if that hop happens to clash and get dropped then then that packet will get delivered to all those repeaters anyway but if I restrict my repeater to 15 hops that message would be lost. It is perfectly possible for any London message to use up 10 or more hops before hitting any of the big hilltop repeaters around London so by restricting hops on your local repeater you are reducing the reliability that meshcore gives.
