Just a heads-up – I’ve been tinkering with something called MeshRank It’s basically a way of giving a bit more visibility into what the mesh is actually doing, without changing how MeshCORE works. No internet routing, no messing with RF, nothing in the message path. It just listens, observes, and feeds back what already happened. Why I built it: • New repeaters often get switched off because it’s hard to tell if they’re helping • People end up spamming test messages just to see if anything’s alive • When stuff doesn’t propagate, there’s no clue why MeshRank gives things like delivery confidence, shows which repeaters are contributing, and highlights where messages are being seen but not going any further. Observers are totally passive — they don’t transmit or interfere in any way. If MeshRank fell over tomorrow, the mesh would carry on exactly the same. I’m looking for a few folks (UK first) who wouldn’t mind running an observer and helping build some early coverage — high ground / urban spots especially useful.
