Tharsis 2026-03-13 5:20 a.m.
If you plug your v3 into your PC and go to the Meshcore flasher page, then click the Console button next to repeater setup and connect to your repeater, then run get prv.key That'll return your private key. Copy and save it somewhere. Plug in your v4, flash it fully, then open the console set prv.key <The key you copied> And you'll be (mostly) golden. You'll need to manually set the name and radio settings again, but you can do that from the normal repeater setup tool also on the flasher page.
