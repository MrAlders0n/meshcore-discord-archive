I was messing around with MeshCore hashtag channels. I learned something new. As hashtag channels are like public channels; once you join the channel you are always "following" the channel. So you will receive new message notification as long as your node is turned on and connected to your mesh. If you leave the local mesh or turn off your node, you will not see any of the messages that were sent to the channel while you were offline. On the other hand, 'Room' servers will store messages (up to 32) and once you sign into the 'ROOM' and leave you node turned on you will receive notification of new messages. If you turn off your node or leave the local mesh you will have to log back into the 'Room' again to receive new messages. Seems like a hashtag channel linked with a Room Server would be a good combination for group or community chat. (edited)
