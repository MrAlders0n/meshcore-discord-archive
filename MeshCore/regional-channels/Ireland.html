I spent some time today looking into sensors for MeshCore. My understanding is that Meshtastic has good support for sensors in that the firmware will detect sensors connected to the I2C bus and automatically send flood messages out every five minutes. However, this didn't sit well with the MeshCore designers due to the amount of traffic created. Therefore, although I believe MeshCore repeaters may well be able to detect the presence of a sensor connected to the I2C bus and take readings from the sensor(s), the values can only be read as telemetry values by logging into the repeater.
