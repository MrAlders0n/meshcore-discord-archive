smatias4 2026-01-27 2:33 p.m.
There are no EU standards for presets, only community suggestions, that may or may not be voluntarily adopted in a particular region. What may apply to a set of countries does not constitute wide adoption, proof is that before the EU/UK narrow became public, other communities were already testing narrow presets, as the default 250k wide (collision with MC albeit with a different SF, plus all the other commercial LoRa services on 868) was not giving good results. We were already doing research for a 868 narrow preset in Portugal when the EU/UK narrow preset suggestion became public.
